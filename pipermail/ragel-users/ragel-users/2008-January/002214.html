Adrian,

In 1.9, use String#ord:

------------------------------------------------------------- String#ord
     str.ord   => integer
------------------------------------------------------------------------
     Return the +Integer+ ordinal of a one-character string.

        "a".ord         #=> 97

So, "foo"[1].ord in 1.9 should be equivalent to "foo"[1] in 1.8.
